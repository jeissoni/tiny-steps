# About Us – Tiny Steps Learning Center

## About Tiny Steps Learning Center

At Tiny Steps Learning Center, we believe early childhood is a time for joyful discovery — a stage where confidence, curiosity, and creativity are built through connection and care.

We provide a family-oriented learning environment that nurtures each child’s physical, social, emotional, and intellectual development. Through the Experience Curriculum, our teachers guide play-based, hands-on exploration that encourages imagination, builds trust, and inspires meaningful growth.

Our spaces are welcoming, our learning is playful, and our purpose is clear: to create a place where every child feels safe, supported, and ready to thrive.

## Our Mission

We nurture a creative learning space where children explore, engage, and grow toward their fullest potential.

## Our Vision

To be a leading influence in early childhood education — known for nurturing confident, creative, and compassionate children through joyful, welcoming, and family-centered learning experiences.

## Our History

Tiny Steps Learning Center opened its doors in 2016 with one goal: to offer high-quality, personalized care that truly meets children where they are. We designed our program to be both thoughtful and playful — blending responsive, developmentally appropriate practices with intentional structure and a personalized approach to support every child’s growth.

Since then, we’ve grown from a single center with room for 30 children to three locations serving up to 180 families across Paterson. Our growth has been guided by the same values we started with: being a safe, welcoming space for families, building strong relationships with children, and always looking for ways to improve.

We embraced tools like Brightwheel early on to strengthen family communication, and we continue to invest in innovation — from the Experience Curriculum in the classroom to automation systems that help our team work smarter. Tiny Steps is still evolving, but our commitment remains the same: to create a nurturing environment where every child is supported, challenged, and inspired to grow.

**Call to Action (optional):** Explore Our Locations

## Our Philosophy

At Tiny Steps, we believe that every child is capable, curious, and full of potential. Children thrive when they are supported as whole individuals — emotionally, socially, physically, and cognitively — in an environment where they feel safe, seen, and inspired to grow.

We create playful, welcoming spaces that reflect the real world of childhood — full of movement, exploration, and wonder. Through the Experience Curriculum, learning becomes hands-on and engaging, inviting creativity, building confidence, and developing character in meaningful ways.

As educators, we see ourselves as partners — with families, with one another, and with each child. We value trust, reflection, and innovation, and we continuously evolve to meet children where they are and support their journey forward with care and intention.

# Why Tiny Steps

## What Makes Us Different

At Tiny Steps Learning Center, we believe childcare should be more than a service — it should be a joyful, meaningful experience for every child and every family. From the way we greet you at the door to how we guide learning in the classroom, everything we do is shaped by care, creativity, and connection. Here’s what sets us apart:

### 1. Creative, Play-Based Learning

Children learn best through play — and our classrooms are full of it. Through the Experience Curriculum, we guide hands-on discovery, sensory-rich activities, movement, music, and storytelling that build essential skills and spark lifelong curiosity.

### 2. Passionate, Caring Educators

Our team is more than qualified — they’re deeply committed. Every educator at Tiny Steps brings warmth, training, and intention into the classroom, supporting each child’s growth with patience, consistency, and joy. All staff receive ongoing training in early childhood development, safety, and curriculum implementation.

### 3. Safe, Welcoming Spaces

Children thrive when they feel safe and secure. That’s why all Tiny Steps locations are clean, well-maintained, and thoughtfully designed. Classrooms are equipped with surveillance systems, and secure access control is in place at all entry points. Families check in and out each day through Brightwheel, adding an extra layer of safety and transparency. Outdoors, children enjoy upgraded, fenced-in play areas built for fun and exploration.

### 4. Flexible & Accessible Childcare

We offer both full-time and part-time schedules to fit your family’s needs. Tiny Steps proudly accepts 4CS and other assistance programs to help make high-quality childcare accessible to more families.

### 5. Healthy Meals & Habits

We serve nutritious meals and snacks daily — all included in tuition. Children also build lifelong habits through guided routines that promote healthy eating, hygiene, and active play.

### 6. Strong Family & Community Connection

Through Brightwheel, parents receive real-time photos, messages, and updates throughout the day. Whether you’re at work or on the go, you’re always connected to what your child is learning and experiencing. Our referral program also rewards families who help grow our Tiny Steps community.

### 7. Enrichment & Special Experiences

Learning doesn’t stop at the classroom door. We offer seasonal events, hands-on learning activities, and enrichment experiences that keep children excited, engaged, and curious throughout the year.

### 8. Competitive Rates & Commitment to Quality

We offer competitive tuition pricing with no surprise fees. As part of our ongoing commitment to excellence, Tiny Steps is actively pursuing a Grow NJ Kids quality rating — further ensuring we meet and exceed state standards in care, education, and family partnership.

# Our Commitment

At Tiny Steps Learning Center, we are deeply committed to providing a safe, healthy, and nurturing space where children can learn, play, and grow with confidence — supported by a team of caring professionals and a culture built on trust, transparency, and joy.

## Safety & Well-Being

Your child’s safety is our top priority. All staff are CPR and First Aid certified and receive ongoing professional development in child safety, supervision, emergency preparedness, and best practices in early education. We believe high-quality care starts with highly trained educators who are always growing in their knowledge and skills. Every classroom and common area features secure access control, monitored surveillance, and daily cleaning protocols. Families check in and out using Brightwheel, giving you peace of mind and full visibility into your child’s day. Outdoors, children enjoy newly updated, fenced-in playgrounds designed for fun and safe exploration.

## Health & Nutrition

We promote lifelong wellness from the earliest years. Children receive balanced, age-appropriate meals and snacks every day — all approved through the Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) and regulated by the New Jersey Department of Agriculture. We accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions with care, and help children build healthy habits through daily routines.

## Personalized Learning & Environments

We create classroom environments that support strong relationships, curiosity, and care. While maintaining licensed teacher-to-child ratios, our educators use a responsive and intentional approach to meet each child’s developmental needs. Our indoor spaces are thoughtfully designed to encourage creativity and discovery, while our outdoor areas promote movement, nature play, and social connection.

## Support for Families & Community

We know that great care supports the whole family. Tiny Steps offers flexible full-time and part-time schedules, extended care options, and proudly accepts 4CS and other childcare assistance programs. We partner with families through real-time communication via Brightwheel, regular check-ins, and family-centered events — building a community that grows together.

## Commitment to Quality & Growth

Our program is grounded in evidence-based practices and meets or exceeds New Jersey’s early childhood standards. We offer competitive tuition with no surprise fees and are actively pursuing a Grow NJ Kids quality rating. As we grow, we remain committed to reflecting, evolving, and delivering the high standards that families deserve.

Tiny Steps is a place where children feel safe, supported, and ready to explore the world.

# FAQs & Policies – Answers to Common Questions and How We Operate

Whether you’re a new parent exploring Tiny Steps or a current family looking for clarification, this section answers our most frequently asked questions and highlights a few key policies that guide how we care for your child and communicate with families.

## General Questions

### 1. What are your hours of operation?

Our centers are open Monday through Friday. You can view center-specific opening and closing times here:

**Center Hours & Contact Information**

For holidays, emergency closures, or early dismissals, refer to our public calendar:

**View Calendar**

### 2. Do you offer part-time care?

Yes. We provide both full-time and part-time care options to support a variety of family needs.

### 3. How do I make tuition payments?

We accept payments through the Brightwheel app, Zelle, 4CS subsidy payments, cash, and money orders.

Payment terms and conditions may vary by child and family situation. Please contact our admin team with questions — we’re happy to work with your specific case.

### 4. What is your illness policy?

Children with symptoms of illness must remain at home until they are well or cleared by a doctor. A medical note may be required.

Full health and exclusion guidelines are shared with enrolled families through our Parent Handbook.

### 5. Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes. All children receive nutritious, age-appropriate meals and snacks each day at no additional cost.

Meals meet CACFP standards and are approved by the New Jersey Department of Agriculture.

### 6. How do I communicate with my child’s teacher?

We use Brightwheel to share messages, photos, daily reports, and real-time updates.

If needed, you can also call your center directly, and an administrator can transfer your call to your child’s classroom.

### 7. Do you accept 4CS or other tuition assistance programs?

Yes. We proudly accept 4CS and other tuition assistance programs supported by the New Jersey Department of Human Services. We follow compliance standards set by the New Jersey Department of Children and Families (DCF), which oversees childcare licensing and support services.

### 8. What ages do you serve?

We welcome children from 6 weeks to 5 years old across all three of our Paterson locations.

During the summer, we also offer programs for children up to 13 years of age. Availability may vary by location and demand.

### 9. Are your staff certified and trained in safety procedures?

Yes. All educators are certified in CPR and First Aid and complete ongoing professional development throughout the year.

We are committed to health, safety, and regulatory compliance under New Jersey DCF licensing standards.

### 10. How do I visit a center or begin enrollment?

You can schedule a tour directly from our homepage or any page where the “Schedule a Tour” button appears. Each center also has a dedicated location page with address details, hours of operation, photos, and contact information:

- McBride Ave – Directions & Info
- Paterson Ave – Directions & Info
- Union Ave – Directions & Info

Once you’re ready, our team will guide you through the application process, document submission, and next steps.
