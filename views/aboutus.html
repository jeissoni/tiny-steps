About Tiny Steps Learning Center

Where Early Childhood Feels Joyful, Safe, and Full of Growth

At Tiny Steps Learning Center, we believe early childhood is a time for joyful discovery — a stage where confidence, curiosity, and creativity are built through connection and care.

We provide a family-oriented learning environment that nurtures each child's physical, social, emotional, and intellectual development. Through the Experience Curriculum, our teachers guide play-based, hands-on exploration that encourages imagination, builds trust, and inspires meaningful growth.

Our spaces are welcoming, our learning is playful, and our purpose is clear: to create a place where every child feels safe, supported, and ready to thrive.

Our Story

How Tiny Steps Grew 🌱

16
2016 — The Beginning

Tiny Steps opened its doors with one clear goal: to offer high-quality, personalized care that truly meets children where they are. Our first center had room for 30 children.

🚀
Early Growth

We designed our program to be both thoughtful and playful — blending responsive, developmentally appropriate practices with intentional structure and a personalized approach to support every child's growth.

📡
Innovation

We embraced tools like Brightwheel early on to strengthen family communication, and we continue to invest in innovation — from the Experience Curriculum to automation systems that help our team work smarter.

🏠
Today

We've grown from a single center to three locations serving up to 180 families across Paterson. Tiny Steps is still evolving, but our commitment remains the same: to create a nurturing environment where every child is supported, challenged, and inspired to grow.

How We Teach

Our Philosophy 💡

At Tiny Steps, we believe that every child is capable, curious, and full of potential. Children thrive when they are supported as whole individuals — emotionally, socially, physically, and cognitively — in an environment where they feel safe, seen, and inspired to grow.

We create playful, welcoming spaces that reflect the real world of childhood — full of movement, exploration, and wonder. As educators, we see ourselves as partners: with families, with one another, and with each child.

❤️

Whole Child Approach

We support every child emotionally, socially, physically, and cognitively — because true learning starts when a child feels safe and seen.

🎠

Play-Based Learning

Through the Experience Curriculum, learning becomes hands-on and engaging — inviting creativity, building confidence, and developing character in meaningful ways.

🤝

Family Partnership

We value trust, reflection, and innovation — and we continuously evolve to meet children where they are and support their journey forward with care and intention.
