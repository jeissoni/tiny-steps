About Tiny Steps

A Joyful Start for Every Child

At Tiny Steps Learning Center, we believe early childhood is a time for joyful discovery — a stage where confidence, curiosity, and creativity grow through connection and care. We provide a family-oriented environment with playful, hands-on learning through the Experience Curriculum, plus real-time updates for families through Brightwheel, so every child feels safe, supported, and ready to thrive.

Why Tiny Steps

What Makes Us Different

Creative, Play-Based Learning

Through the Experience Curriculum, we guide hands-on discovery, sensory-rich activities, movement, music, and storytelling that build skills and spark curiosity.

Passionate, Caring Educators

Our team brings warmth, training, and intention to every classroom — with ongoing professional development in early childhood, safety, and curriculum.

Safe, Welcoming Spaces

Clean, thoughtfully designed classrooms; secure access and monitored spaces; Brightwheel check-in/out; fenced outdoor play for safe exploration.

Flexible & Accessible Childcare

Full-time and part-time schedules to fit your family’s needs. We proudly accept 4CS and other assistance programs to make high-quality care more accessible.

Healthy Meals & Habits

Nutritious meals and snacks daily — all included in tuition. Guided routines that promote healthy eating, hygiene, and active play.

Strong Family & Community Connection

Real-time photos and messages through Brightwheel, plus a referral program that rewards families who help grow our Tiny Steps community.

Enrichment & Special Experiences

Seasonal events, hands-on learning activities, and enrichment experiences that keep children excited, engaged, and curious throughout the year.

Competitive Rates & Commitment to Quality

Competitive tuition with no surprise fees. We’re actively pursuing a Grow NJ Kids quality rating — meeting and exceeding state standards in care, education, and family partnership.
